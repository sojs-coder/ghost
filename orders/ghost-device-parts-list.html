What you skip on the breadboard

The nRF52840-DK already includes a 3.3V regulator, so you don't need a standalone LDO for the breadboard phase. The on-board J-Link means no external programmer is needed. The production MCP73831 charger is replaced by the standalone Adafruit module — same IC, easier wiring.

What changes for PCB

The DK will be replaced by a bare u-blox ANNA-B112 or Nordic nRF52840 module. The Adafruit breakouts get replaced by the discrete ICs (MCP73831, MAX17048, W25Q128JVSIQ) in their production packages. The NeoPixel swaps for a 4-pin RGB LED.

Buy from Mouser or DigiKey

For the nRF52840-DK specifically, avoid Amazon — clones exist and the on-board J-Link is a key differentiator. Mouser and DigiKey are interchangeable; check live stock and shipping time. Adafruit and SparkFun ship direct and are the right source for breakouts.
